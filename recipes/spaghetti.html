Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

So good...so cheap..

Spaghetti aglio e olio means spaghetti with garlic and olive oil. This stuff comes out to like $1 per meal and it's pretty good.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Calabrian chili
  • Breadcrumbs

Steps

  1. Heat up oil and garlic in a skillet
  2. Cook the pasta
  3. Add the pasta to the oil/garlic
  4. Add the chili and parsley
  5. Eat it
