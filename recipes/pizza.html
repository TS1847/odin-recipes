Pizza Recipe

Easy, simple, homemade pizza

Most people think you can't make great pizza at home. Well they're mostly right. Really good, crispy pizza requires an oven that gets really hot. But... one thing you can do is use a cast iron skillet, because those things get hot as shit. The shape of a standard cast iron skillet also works well to give your homemade pie a good shape... you know... round.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven hot as shit
  2. Put the skillet in to heat up
  3. Mess around with the dough
  4. Take the skillet out and put the dough in
  5. Put the sauce and cheese on top
  6. Let cook until it looks done
  7. Put the basil on for the gggram
  8. Crush the zahh
